About Us

We work to build a better future

B1 Property Group is a fully insured construction service provider headquartered in St Pauls Square, Birmingham, serving the West Midlands region. We work with developers, estate agents, landlords, insurance firms, management companies, as well as domestic clients. Our services encompass building and maintenance repairs, complete home refurbishments, heating and plumbing installations, electrical work, fire safety measures, decorating, and plastering. B1 Property Group prides itself on exceptional customer communication and effective management of client expectations
